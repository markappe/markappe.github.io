--- layout: archive title: "Research" permalink: /research/ author_profile: true --- In my dissertation, I study the antecedents and consequences of issue changes of social movement organizations using quantitive and computational methods. With Phillip Brandt, Jeremy Kuhnle, and Henning Hillmann, I investigate labor market careers of immigrants in the German labor market. With Jacob Habinek, I currently investigate how sociologists use theoretical models in the absence of theoretical consensus. Together with Pavel Dimitrov Chachev, I study collaboration patterns of YouTubers.
